About Us

Full local bar and Juices.

Welcome to Salmat Local Bar and Juices! Nestled in the hear, Salmat stands as a beacon of warmth and camaraderie in our bustling community. With its inviting ambiance, stellar drinks, and friendly staff, it has become a beloved watering hole for locals and visitors alike. Stepping into Salmat, you're immediately greeted by the cozy atmosphere that feels like a home away from home. The walls adorned with memorabilia and vintage decor evoke a sense of nostalgia, inviting patrons to unwind and escape the hectic pace of everyday life. At Salmat, we take pride in our craft cocktails and freshly squeezed juices, expertly mixed by our talented bartenders. Whether you're in the mood for a classic Old Fashioned or a refreshing fruit juice blend, there's something on our menu to tantalize every palate. And for beer enthusiasts, we offer a curated selection of local brews on tap, ensuring a taste of the region with every sip. What's a night out without some live music to set the mood? Salmat regularly hosts talented local musicians, creating an unforgettable experience for patrons. Whether it's a bluesy guitar solo or the soulful notes of a jazz saxophone, our stage comes alive with the sounds of talented artists, adding an extra layer of magic to your evening. More than just a place to grab a drink, Salmat serves as a hub of community activity. From charity events and trivia nights to game watch parties, there's always something happening at our bar. It's where friends gather to catch up, strangers become acquaintances, and memories are made. Join us at Salmat Local Bar and Juices! Whether you're stopping by for a quick drink after work or settling in for a night of revelry with friends, Salmat welcomes you with open arms. We'll save you a seat at the bar!
